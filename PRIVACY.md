# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 2026**

G-Notes is a local-first notes application. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled.

## Data Collection

G-Notes does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. 

- All notes, categories, and app data are stored **locally** on your device in a SQLite database.
- No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting tools are included.
- No account registration is required.
- No cookies are used.
- No data is sent to third-party services.

## Data Storage

Your data is stored in a SQLite database file located at:

- **Linux**: `~/.config/g-notes/g-notes.db`
- **macOS**: `~/Library/Application Support/g-notes/g-notes.db`
- **Windows**: `%APPDATA%/g-notes/g-notes.db`

This data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly export it (File > Export Notes) or share the database file manually.

## External Links

If you click a link within a note, the URL is opened in your default system browser. G-Notes does not track which links are clicked.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be included with the app update.
